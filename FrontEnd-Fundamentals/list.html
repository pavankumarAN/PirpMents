Lemon rice is a indian famous dish, espicically in south india. This food is easy to cook and cooked with healthy ingradients

Lemon Rice

    Ingradients

  • Rice
  • Lemon
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Spices

    Process

  1. Cook rice in a cooker
  2. Put some oil in the bowl and keep it on the stove
  3. Add spices as per taste
  4. Add onion pieces to the bowl
  5. After mix with the rice and stir for few minutes

Food will looks like rice with yellow color and onion, spicey and tasty.
